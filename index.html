About Our Institution

Raza Darul Ifta wal Qaza serves as a beacon of Shariah guidance for the Ummah. Our mission is to bridge the gap between classical Fiqh and contemporary challenges.

Led by qualified Muftis, we specialize in Ibaadat, Muamalaat (finance), and Nikah/Talaq matters, ensuring every response is grounded in the Quran, Sunnah, and the consensus of the Ahlus Sunnah wal Jama'ah.
