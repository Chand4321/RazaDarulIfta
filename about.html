Light of Knowledge

Founded with the vision to provide authentic, moderate, and well-researched Islamic legal opinions (Fatwa), **Raza Darul Ifta wal Qaza** stands as a sanctuary for those seeking clarity in an ever-changing world.

Our methodology is rooted in the classical **Ahlus Sunnah wal Jama'ah** tradition, specifically adhering to the Hanafi school of jurisprudence while respecting the diversity of the four valid Madhahib.
